NLP: European Parliament Speeches + Labelled Reddit Discourse

TL;DR: Two NLP case studies on large unstructured text: mapping political discourse in European Parliament debates and analysing semantic structure in labelled Reddit mental-health discussions.

Projects

  • European Parliament debates (106,598 speeches): TF-IDF vectorisation, UMAP/PCA visualisation, and LDA topic modelling to uncover themes and compare similarity across parties/roles.
  • Labelled Reddit dataset (800 posts): TF-IDF + LSA (SVD) for semantic factors, DBSCAN clustering on reduced representations, and keyness analysis to test whether label categories separate cleanly.

Key takeaway

Political groups share administrative language but diverge on issue-specific topics; Reddit mental-health discourse appears more continuous than neatly separable clusters, suggesting labels can blur in practice.

Tools

TF-IDF, LDA, UMAP/PCA, LSA/SVD, DBSCAN, keyness analysis.
